In other news, I found a new way to cook fish, and the dish has taken over this week's dinner menu. The technique involves lining a pan with butter, cold fish, and diced shallots and covering the pan with a fluted piece of parchment paper. You dump in just enough white wine to go halfway up the fish, and simmer gently until the fish is cooked through. As a bonus, it makes your whole house smell amazing.
